01

Introduction

This Refund and Cancellation Policy governs all payments made to Webrox Studio for digital services including, but not limited to, website development, mobile application development, digital marketing, website maintenance, and related technical services.

By engaging the services of Webrox Studio, the Client acknowledges that they have read, understood, and agreed to this Refund and Cancellation Policy.

02

Advance Payment Requirement

To initiate any project or service engagement, the Client is required to pay an advance payment as specified in the project proposal, quotation, or service agreement.

Purpose: This advance payment secures project scheduling, resource allocation, and commencement of work by the Company.
03

Non-Refundable Advance Payment

🚫 Strictly Non-Refundable: All advance payments made to Webrox Studio are strictly non-refundable under any circumstances.

Once the advance payment has been received and the project has been confirmed, the Company will begin allocating resources, planning, and development activities. Therefore, such payments shall not be eligible for refund under any circumstances.

04

Project Cancellation by Client

If the Client chooses to cancel the project after signing the agreement or after work has commenced:

  • The advance payment shall remain non-refundable.
  • The Client may be required to pay for the work completed up to the date of cancellation.
  • Any costs incurred by the Company for third-party services, tools, hosting, or licenses shall also be payable by the Client.
05

Cancellation by the Company

Webrox Studio reserves the right to terminate or suspend a project or service engagement under the following circumstances:

  • Failure of the Client to make required payments within the agreed timeline
  • Repeated delays in providing necessary project information or approvals
  • Engagement in illegal, unethical, or abusive conduct
  • Violation of contractual terms or applicable laws
⚠ Please Note: In such cases, any payments already made shall remain non-refundable.
06

Refund Eligibility

Refunds may only be considered under exceptional circumstances at the sole discretion of Webrox Studio, such as:

  • When the Company determines that it is unable to deliver the agreed services due to internal limitations.
💡 If Applicable: In such cases, any refund issued shall be partial and proportionate to the work not yet performed, and administrative or operational costs may be deducted.
07

Digital Service Nature

Due to the nature of digital services — including design work, software development, technical consulting, and digital marketing — services once initiated are considered non-returnable and non-refundable.

Why? Time, expertise, and resources invested in digital services cannot be reversed once work has commenced. Unlike physical goods, digital work represents irreversible effort.
08

Third-Party Service Costs

Any third-party costs paid on behalf of the Client including, but not limited to:

  • Domain registrations
  • Web hosting services
  • Software licenses
  • Plugins, APIs, or integrations
  • Advertising platform budgets

…are non-refundable and remain subject to the policies of the respective third-party providers.

09

Dispute Resolution

In the event of any dispute related to payments, refunds, or cancellation, both parties agree to attempt to resolve the matter amicably through mutual discussion.

If resolution cannot be reached, the matter shall be governed by the applicable laws of India and subject to the jurisdiction of competent courts within India.

First Step: We always encourage open communication. Please reach out to us at contact.webroxstudio@gmail.com before escalating any matter.
10

Policy Updates

Webrox Studio reserves the right to modify or update this Refund and Cancellation Policy at any time. Updated versions shall become effective immediately upon publication on the official website.
